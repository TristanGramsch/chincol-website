ChatSR: 311 Service Request System

A showcase of our project management capabilities for local government

What is ChatSR?

ChatSR is an innovative 311-style system designed for local government agencies. It allows citizens to easily report non-emergency issues using natural language processing to understand and route requests efficiently.

Key Features

  • AI-powered natural language understanding
  • Multi-channel support (web, mobile, SMS)
  • Real-time status updates
  • Automated routing to the right department
  • Analytics dashboard for city officials

Note: ChatSR is a showcase project demonstrating our capabilities. Contact us to learn how we can implement similar solutions for your needs.
